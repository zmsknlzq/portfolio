I am proud to be the vice president of Pedone, an association for artistic and cultural exchange between China and the rest of the world, focussing on Italy. Pedone was founded in 2015 by Tsinghua and Brera alumnus Zhiying Li, our beloved president.

We have since been very active in fulfilling our mission of fostering the artistic and cultural exchanges between Chinese artists, art lovers, and literati on the one hand, and their foreign counterparts, focussing on Italy, where we are based in Greater Milan.

Pedone has so far been successful in letting the European public get acquainted with such Chinese masters as painter Fangyuan Li, with two monographic exhibitions (on the left is the video recap of 2024's one), and a third to come in 2025, as well as enabling the Chinese audience to recognize the talent of such Western artists as Italian Sabrina Aureli, Peruvian Marcia Zegarra, and many more.

Pedone Association for the Intercultural Exchange provides not only art curation and event organisation but also a wide range of services to facilitate the integration of Chinese artists and art students in the Italian society, such as Courses of Art, Design, Italian, Music, and Python and Housing solutions in Lombardy.
